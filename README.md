## Rationale
Jobseekers in the Philippines, particularly fresh graduates and first-time applicants, face significant challenges during job interviews despite having academic qualifications. Many struggle with confidence, communication skills, and effectively presenting their technical knowledge due to limited exposure to real interview environments. This project proposes an AI-Powered Mock Interview and Job Readiness Simulation System designed specifically for IT jobseekers.

## Objectives
- Simulate realistic IT job interviews using AI-generated, role-specific questions, including coding challenges.
- Enable voice-based responses, allowing users to answer interview questions verbally.
- Evaluate communication skills, confidence, and technical accuracy, including code logic and explanation.
- Provide automated, actionable feedback and improvement suggestions to guide users.
- Enhance job readiness and employability of fresh graduates and first-time IT jobseekers.

## Key Features (4W1H)
- **What:** AI-Powered Mock Interview & Job Readiness Simulation System.
- **Why:** To address lack of interview experience, poor communication, and difficulty presenting technical knowledge.
- **Who:** Fresh Graduates, IT Jobseekers, College Students.
- **How:**
  - AI-Generated Interview Questions tailored to expertise.
  - Speech-to-Text for capturing spoken answers.
  - Text-to-Speech for AI interviewer voice.
  - Automated Feedback System.
  - AI-Generated Scenario-based Code Hands-on.

## Major Activities
1. **Research & Planning:** Gather data from target users.
2. **System Design:** Plan interview flow, speech interaction, and feedback generation.
3. **System Development:**
   - Web application for user profiling.
   - AI interview module (role-specific questions).
   - Speech-to-text and text-to-speech integration.
   - Automated feedback system.
   - Hands-on coding scenarios with AI feedback loop.
4. **Testing & Improvements:** Usability testing and feedback collection.
5. **Launch & Implementation:** Pilot use among graduating students.

## Expected Outputs
- **AI Mock Interview Application:** Web app for practice.
- **Speech-Based Interview Feature:** Simulates real conditions.
- **Automated Feedback System:** Evaluates communication and technical accuracy.
- **Interview Performance Report:** Summary of strengths and weaknesses.
